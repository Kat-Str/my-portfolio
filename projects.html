Weather app

Weather app provides current weather conditions and forecasts for a user's location or any other city in the world. The app fetches data from an API and displays information such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation on a user-friendly interface.

World clock

World clock app is a time management tool that displays the current time in different time zones around the world, allowing users to quickly and easily see what time it is in different locations. It can help users stay on schedule and coordinate with others in different time zones. A world clock app can be a useful addition to any productivity or time management toolkit.

Gardening app

Gardening app that helps users keep track of their plants by allowing them to set watering schedules, receive reminders, and access information on optimal growing conditions for various plants.
